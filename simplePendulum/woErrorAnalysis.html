This webpage uses the Runge-Kutta-Fehlberg fourth-order method with fifth-order error checking to approximate the solution to the problem of the simple pendulum: $$\dfrac{d^2 \theta}{dt^2} = -\dfrac{g}{l} \cos{\theta}$$ where g is the acceleration due to gravity in metres per second squared, l is the length of the pendulum in metres, θ is the angle from the positive x axis (in radians) and t is the time in seconds.
